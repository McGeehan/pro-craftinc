  • The Mill

        Pro-Craft Inc's headquarters have an interesting history. It was once used as Cotton Yarn Mill during the 1800s.

        James Irving erected the mill that houses Pro-Craft in 1860. Then, in 1878, Irving's company was incorporated under the law of the State of Pennsylvania as the Irving & Leiper Manufacturing Company. There, cotton yarns and fabrics were manufactured and sold.

         Casciato Electric completed electrical maintenance work in the 1950s. The Mill was built along the creek and had water run through the building where a water wheel powered the line shafts that allowed the machines to operate.
